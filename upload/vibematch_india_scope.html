Why India is actually the bigger opportunity
👥 600 million people under 25. India has more young people than the entire population of the EU. Even capturing 0.1% of that market is 600,000 users.
💸 Indian youth spend heavily on experiences. Post-pandemic, spending on going out, events and socialising has grown 35%+ year on year among urban youth. This is not a market cutting back.
📱 UPI makes payments frictionless. India processes more digital payments than any country on earth. Your entire payment layer — tickets, drinks, security — all works instantly via UPI/Razorpay with zero card friction.
🚫 Zero direct competition. Skiddle doesn't operate in India. Meetup is generic and declining. Nobody owns the small gathering space in India. You'd be entering an empty room.
🛡️ Safety sells. Especially for women in Indian cities, a verified, approval-gated, security-bookable party platform is not just a nice-to-have — it's the reason they'd use your app over WhatsApp group chaos.

Step 7 of 9 · Guest + Host

Arrive + QR check-in

Guest shows QR code on their phone. Host scans it with the app — spot confirmed, pre-orders flagged, drinks handed over. The whole check-in takes about three seconds.

Nobody gets in without a valid QR. The host doesn't have to awkwardly turn someone away — the app does it.

Step 9 of 9 · Host

Full admin control

The host is in charge at all times. Approve or reject anyone before they join. Remove someone on the night with automatic full refund. Toggle joiners, menu, and approval on or off instantly.

✅
Approval gate
When turned on, every new joiner needs host approval before their payment processes. Nobody gets in uninvited.
🚫
Remove + auto-refund
Remove any guest with a tap. App picks a reason, full refund fires instantly, guest is removed from chat. No awkward confrontation.
🎛️
Live party controls
Pause new joiners, close the menu, adjust the guest cap — all in real time without contacting anyone.
